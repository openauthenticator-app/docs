Features

Everything you need.
Nothing more.

Designed to be simple, reliable, and respectful of your data. With no compromise on security.

🔐

Standard format

Compatible with all services that support two-factor authentication: Google, GitHub, Discord, and thousands of others.

☁️

Synchronization

Because it would be a shame to lose access to your accounts when changing phones. You can also self-host the backend if you want to (more info).

🛡️

Private data

Your data is encrypted before being transmitted using a key derived from a master password.

📱

Cross-platform

One project, all platforms. Android, iOS, macOS, Windows, and Linux — built with Flutter.

🌍

Translations

The app is multilingual and translated by the community. Join the contributors and help make it accessible to everyone.

⚡

Ultra-lightweight

No bloat, no unnecessary permissions. The app does what it says, and nothing else, quickly.
